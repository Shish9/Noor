## Full description (Play, max 4000 chars)
```
Noor is a calm, beautifully crafted companion for the Qur'an, du'a, and daily
remembrance — designed to feel like light.

• THE HOLY QUR'AN
Read all 114 surahs with elegant Amiri Quran typography. Pick up exactly where
you left off, bookmark verses, and adjust the Arabic font size to your comfort.
Listen with per-ayah audio from 15 world-renowned reciters — including Mishary
Alafasy, Abdul Rahman Al-Sudais, Abdul Basit, Al-Husary, Al-Minshawi and more —
with audio that continues in the background and on your lock screen.

• DU'AS & ADHKAR
Authentic supplications organized by what your heart needs — anxiety, gratitude,
protection, forgiveness, sleep, travel, morning & evening adhkar, and more. Each
du'a includes the Arabic, transliteration, translation, and source.

• DHIKR COUNTER
A tactile tasbih counter for SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar,
istighfar, salawat and more — with a daily total to keep your tongue moist with
His remembrance.

• PRAYER TIMES & QIBLA
Accurate prayer times calculated for your location, with a sun-arc view of the
day and an animated Qibla compass that points you toward the Ka'bah.

• GENTLE REMINDERS
Soft, optional du'a notifications throughout your day — your way, on your
schedule.

• THOUGHTFUL DESIGN
A serene "midnight & gold" aesthetic, premium typography, and smooth, quiet
motion. Fully bilingual: English and Arabic, with complete right-to-left
support — every screen, every label.

• PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account. No analytics. No data collection. Everything — your bookmarks,
reading position, streak, and settings — stays on your device. Your location is
used only on-device to compute prayer times and is never sent anywhere.

May Noor be a source of light, calm, and closeness in your day. 🤍
```

## Data Safety form (Play — the important one)

**Does your app collect or share any user data?**
→ The app does **not collect or share** data with you (the developer) or third
parties. Choose accordingly:

- **Data collection:** No data collected
  - Rationale: name, bookmarks, settings, coordinates are stored **only on the
    device** and never transmitted to you. Under Play's definition, on-device-only
    storage is **not** "collection."
- **Data sharing:** None
- **Location:** used on-device for prayer times; NOT collected/sent → declare
  **not collected**.
- **Encryption in transit:** Yes (Quran/audio fetched over HTTPS)
- **Users can request data deletion:** N/A (no data held) — note "all data is
  on-device; uninstalling removes it."

> If the Play reviewer flags location permission: explain in the review notes
> that location is requested only to compute prayer times locally via the
> `adhan` algorithm and is never stored off-device.
