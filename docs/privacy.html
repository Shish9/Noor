PRIVACY POLICY

Noor — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

In short: Noor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any server. There is no account system and no analytics. Everything you do stays on your device.

What the app accesses

Location (optional)

Used on your device to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction. Coordinates are cached locally so the app doesn't re-ask each launch. Your location is never sent to us — we operate no servers. You may deny location; the app falls back to default timings.

Notifications

Gentle du'a reminders you opt into are scheduled locally on your device. No push server is involved.

Storage / Photos

Only when you tap “Save” on a shareable card — the image is written to your gallery at that moment. We never read your photos.

Internet

Used to download Qur'an text and recitation audio from public Islamic content providers: alquran.cloud (text), everyayah.com & mp3quran.net (audio), and Google Fonts (typefaces). These providers may log standard request metadata such as IP address, as any web server does.
