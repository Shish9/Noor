# Privacy Policy — Noor

_Last updated: 2026_

Noor ("the app", "we") is a Quran reading, du'a, dhikr, and prayer-times
companion. We designed Noor to respect your privacy. This policy explains
what the app accesses and why.

## Summary

- **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.**
  Noor has no account system and no analytics SDK.
- Everything you do (bookmarks, reading position, streak, dhikr counts,
  your name, settings) is stored **only on your device**.
- The app makes network requests only to fetch Quran text and recitation
  audio from public Islamic content providers.

## What the app accesses

### Location (optional)
- **Why:** to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for where
  you are.
- **How:** your device's coordinates are used **on-device** to compute prayer
  times. Coordinates are cached locally so the app doesn't ask every launch.
- **We never send your location to our servers** (we don't operate any).
- You can deny location permission — the app falls back to default timings.

### Notifications
- **Why:** to deliver the gentle du'a reminders you opt into.
- Notifications are scheduled **locally on your device**. No push server is
  involved.

### Storage / Photos (only when you share)
- **Why:** when you tap "Save" on a shareable story card, the image is
  written to your gallery.
- Used only at the moment you choose to save; we don't read your photos.

### Internet
- **Why:** to download Quran verse text and recitation audio.
- Third-party content providers used:
  - **alquran.cloud** — Quran text + translations
  - **everyayah.com** and **mp3quran.net** — recitation audio
  - **fonts.google.com** — Amiri Quran / Cormorant / Manrope fonts
- These providers may log standard request metadata (e.g. IP address) as any
  web server does. Please refer to their respective privacy policies.

## Data we store on your device

| Data | Purpose | Leaves device? |
|---|---|---|
| Your name | Personalized greeting | No |
| Bookmarks, favorites | Save verses/du'as | No |
| Last-read position | Resume reading | No |
| Reading streak, dhikr counts | Progress tracking | No |
| Settings (language, font size, reciter, notifications) | Preferences | No |
| Cached coordinates | Prayer times | No |

You can erase all of it at any time by clearing the app's data or
uninstalling.

## Children's privacy

Noor does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change and the new
version will ship with the app.
